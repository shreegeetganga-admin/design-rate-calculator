# Design Freelance Rate Calculator
Free tool for beginner freelance designers to calculate 
their minimum hourly and project rates.

## What it does
Enter your salary goal, expenses, and working hours. 
Get your minimum rate in INR, USD, or your local currency.

## Who it's for
Freelance designers in their first year who are undercharging.
